Jump Back - jump back a fixed amount of time in playback timeline (left arrow key)
 - double tap

Jump Forward - jump forward a fix amount of time in playback timeline (right arrow key)
 - double tap

Speed Up - faster playback (up arrow key)
 - swipe up

Slow Down - slower playback (down arrow key)
 - swipe down

Font Bigger - make font bigger (+ or = keys)
 - swipe right

Font Smaller - make font smaller (- key)
 - swipe left

Pause Toggle - pause/unpause playback (spacebar)
 - swipe up or down
